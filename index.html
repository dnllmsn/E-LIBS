LIBRARY VISION

The Pateros Technological College Library supports the learning potential of the students and to enhance the teaching and research capabilities of faculty. The library acquires, organizes, make accessible and preserves sources of knowledge in all formats.

LIBRARY MISSION

The Pateros Technological College Library will be user-friendly and service oriented manner which will consider us the preferred provider of learning and research information by the students and faculty.

LIBRARY OBJECTIVES

To realize the vision-mission of the institution, the PTC Library hopes to pursue excellence in Technical-Vocational education by providing relevant & quality library materials that will:

  • Meet the needs and interests of the students, the faculty & other personnel.
  • Support the learning potentials of the students.
  • Enhance the teaching & research capabilities of the faculty.
  • Instill love of country & appreciation of our cultural heritage.
  • Enhance moral and spiritual values.
